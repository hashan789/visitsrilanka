             <h2>bopath falls</h2>
             
             <p>bopath ella is one of the most famous and unique tourist attractions in sri lanka.this waterfall is located in kuruwita area in ratnapura district.the height of this waterfall
                 is about feet 90(30 m).the waterfall travels along a narrow path with rocks and then widens to form the shape of a "bo" leaf.because of that,this waterfall is known as bopath falls.</p>

             <h2>pahantudawa falls</h2>
             
             <p>pahanthudawa falls starts from the top of hawagala mountain.it is not wrong to say that pahanthudawa is the best natural aesthetic creation of the environment.this waterfall is shaped

             <h2>bambarakanda falls</h2>
             
             <p>bambarakanda falls(bambarakele falls) is the tallest waterfall in sri lanka.this height is about 263 m(feet 863).it is the 299<sup>th</sup> tallest waterfall in the world.this is located in the
                 kalupahana area of badulla.it is formed by a small stream which is a tributary of the walawe river.bambarakanda falls is found in a forest full of pine trees.</p>
             </article>
