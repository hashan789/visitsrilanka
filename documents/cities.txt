                  <h2>kandy</h2>
            
                <p>kandy is the capital of the central province of sri lanka and is located in the kandy district.it was the last capital of the ancient king's era of srilanka.
                    kandy is both an administrative and religious city.it was declared a world heritage site by UNESCO in 1988.the city of kandy,which has a cool climate, is a popular 
                    destination for both local and foreign tourists and buddhists alike.temple of the tooth,bhairavakanda temple,kandy lake are some such places.the kandy lake and the temple 
                    of the tooth bring a unique uniqueness to the city of kandy.</p>

                <h2>galle</h2>
                
                <p>the city of galle in sri lanka is located in the galle district.galle is the best example of a fortified city built by the Portuguese architectural styles and native
                    traditions.galle city,which is a world heritage site and has a warm climate,is home to several tourist attractions such as galle fort,galle harbor and rumassala.here,
                    the main tourist attraction of galle,galle fort is the largest remaining fortress in asia built by European occupiers.the city was extensively fortified by the dutch 
                    during the 17<sup>th</sup> century from 1649 onwards.and also,the galle international cricket stadium is located here and is popular among cricket fans all over the world.</p>
